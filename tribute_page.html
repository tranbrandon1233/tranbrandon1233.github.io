About Albert Einstein

Albert Einstein is one of, if not the most, renowned physicist of all time and made great strides in helping us understand the universe. Einstein was a German physicist who formulated the theory of relativity. His brilliant explanation of the photoelectric effect earned him a Nobel Prize for Physics in 1921. As a result of his work, Einstein became one of the most distinguished scientists of the century. In addition to his scientific endeavors, Einstein was also a pacifist. His Jewish ancestry forced him to immigrate from Germany to the United States in December 1932 as the Nazis gained power in his home country.

Early Life

Einstein was born on March 14, 1879 in Germany. He attended he Swiss Federal Institute of Technology in Zurich and worked as a patent clerk.
